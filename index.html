Tax model: Savings interest taxed at 22% annually (kapitalinntekt). Index fund gains taxed at 37.84% on realized annual gains — the Norwegian rate for equity funds in a regular taxable account (22% × oppjusteringsfaktor 1.72). An aksjesparekonto (ASK) defers tax until withdrawal and would show a larger fund advantage. Return assumption: MSCI World trailing 10-year nominal ~10.5%/yr. Savings rate held fixed — it is a spot rate and likely to fall. Inflation: Real values are nominal values deflated by (1 + rate)year. Fund fees (~0.1–0.2%/yr) are not modelled. Not financial advice.
